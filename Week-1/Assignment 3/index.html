Nate Ruess

Nate Ruess (born February 26, 1982) is an American singer and songwriter.
He is best known as the founder and lead singer of the indie pop band Fun, and of The Format.
As of 2015, he also performs as a solo musician

From 2009 to 2013, Ruess dated fashion designer Rachel Antonoff, the sister of Fun guitarist Jack Antonoff.
Rachel and Ruess both provided backing vocals for the song "Dakota" from the 2007 album Trampoline by Jack's band Steel Train.
Later, Rachel sang on some Fun songs and appeared in the video for "We Are Young".
Rachel and Ruess collaborated on The Ally Coalition, an activist project to raise awareness of LGBTQ issues.
Ruess began dating English fashion designer Charlotte Ronson in March 2014.
